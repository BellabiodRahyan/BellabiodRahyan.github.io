Bachelor’s Internship - Institute of Low Temperature Science (Sapporo, Japan)

Location: Institute of Low Temperature Science, Sapporo, Japan

Period: May 2024 – August 2024 (3 months)

Supervisor: Prof. Yuki Kimura

Project: Investigated material evolution in the universe from a microscopic point of view, specifically studying nanoscale nucleation and growth of Ca oxide and Si oxide.
